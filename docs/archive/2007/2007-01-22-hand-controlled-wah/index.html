    Hand-controlled Wah

    Michele Pasin 路 路 路 January 22, 2007 路 1 min read

    News for all the guitarists and music technology lovers: the Source Audio Hot Hand motion control for guitar effects. An innovative way to control the guitar sound in a theremin-like manner! Amazon lets you buy it for only 300$ now! Watch the video below to see it in action...

    The Hot Hand system is a patent pending technology that uses micro-machined motion sensors to translate movement and position into control signals for use in guitar effects. The system provides an unprecedented level of control and expressiveness and offers the artist a new way of manipulating effects.
